About

The Bird Cage is the largest specialty bird retailer of services and solutions for the lifetime needs of birds. At The Bird Cage, we love birds, and we believe birds make us better people. Every day with every connection, The Bird Cage passionate online website helps bring bird parents closer to their bird so they can live more fulfilled lives. This mission impacts everything we do for our customers and how we give back to our communities.
